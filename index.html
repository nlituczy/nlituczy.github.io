I am a designer who specializes at creating engaging experiences and data driven ideas. Every project begins by collaboratively mapping out the problem set, ethnographies, and applicable pattern libraries. My design process immediately starts with iterative prototyping to capture user feedback and data points as soon as possible. I feel that by constantly testing, key insights can be found much sooner giving the project a focused personality early on. My spare time is largely spent looking for new ideas and experiences. I don’t believe that product design is only a creative process. I think that is an intersection of many different skills. In order to understand this I seek to understand the world we live in and the products we design for it.
